Privacy Policy

FileCaboNet is an internal tool used by The Gem Shop to connect to QuickBooks Online for accounting automation.

Data we access

When authorized, the app can access QuickBooks Online accounting data (e.g., customers, terms, invoices, sales receipts, and classes) for the connected company.

How we use data

Data is used only to view and update accounting records for the benefit of the connected company.

Sharing

We do not sell or share QuickBooks data with third parties. Access is limited to authorized users of The Gem Shop.

Retention

OAuth tokens are stored securely and can be revoked by disconnecting the app in QuickBooks Online.
